SUPPORT & TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Q: How do I update wholesale prices?
A: Edit output/PRICING_TEMPLATE.csv and run: node apply_pricing.js

Q: Can I use a different pricing formula?
A: Yes - edit lines 44-46 in apply_pricing.js to modify the multipliers

Q: What if I need to re-scrape the data?
A: Run: node full-scrape.js

Q: Are the product images permanent?
A: Images are hosted on United Fabrics servers. For permanence, download
   them locally and update URLs in the CSV.

Q: Can I import to other platforms?
A: Yes! Use woocommerce_products_full.csv for WooCommerce or
   shopify_products_full.csv for Shopify

Q: What if a product has errors during import?
A: Check that the CSV is UTF-8 encoded and that image URLs are accessible.
   Re-run the scraper if data becomes stale.
